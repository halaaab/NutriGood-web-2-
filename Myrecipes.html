Banana bread
  • 3 bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup oat flour (gluten-free)
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  1. Heat the oven at 180°C
  2. Mix the wet ingredients
  3. Add the dry ingredients
  4. Bake for 35–40 minutes
