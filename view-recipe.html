Kale salad

Your daily guide to healthy living

Details

Category: Salads / Healthy
Description: A fresh and nutritious kale salad, a light lemon dressing, and a mix of flavorful toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kale washed and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 apple, sliced (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  • Prepare the kale remove the thick stems and chop the leaves into bite-size pieces.
  • Place the kale in a large bowl, add olive oil and a pinch of salt, then massage with your hands.
  • Make the dressing
  • Add lemon juice, garlic (if using), black pepper, and adjust salt to taste.
  • Add toppings
